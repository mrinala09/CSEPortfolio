Recipes of Tomorrow

Our Final Product was ultimately our cookbook app! However, instead of being like a traditional cookbook that a user can look at a database for recipes. Our program takes a user input for any ingredients they desire, there is no limit to the specifics of the ingredients and the amount they can have. Then our product displays recipes along with instructions for it. Making the search significantly more efficient. This is a web app, meaning there is URL for it and our program runs on a website that can be accessed by anyone. Hence the use of a UI with flask and mvc.
